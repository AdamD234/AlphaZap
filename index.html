AlphaZap

The rules:

  • A random word is selected as the challenge word.
  • When there's a pair of the same letter they get zapped.
  • Add your own words to the challenge word.
  • Zap all the letters to win.
  • Once you've zapped one challenge word, another one is selected or you can choose your own.

Features:

  • Undo your last turn.
  • Restart with the current words.
  • Restart with a different word.
  • Choose your own starting word (letters only, no spaces).
  • Alphabetize the current list of letters.
